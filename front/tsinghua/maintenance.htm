Tsinghua University - Yue's Group

Our group is a team of faculty, postdocs, graduates and undergraduates who work together in the field of computer vision, medical image analysis, machine learning and its applications. In computer vision, our research addresses the fundamental challenges and applications of 3D visual perception and cognition. In medical image analysis, we focus on medical image segmentation, registration, and computer-aided diagnosis. For machine learning, we are working on complex data correlation modelling and optimization, especially deep neural networks and graph/hypergraph based learning methods.

We are currently looking for talented undergraduates, graduates and postdocs to join our teams. Welcome to join our big family!
